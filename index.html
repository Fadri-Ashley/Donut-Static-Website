Jelly Donut

A jelly donut is a donut with a fruit preserve filling. Varieties include the German Berliner, the Polish paczki, the Jewish sufganiyot, the Southern European krafne and the Italian bombolone.

Glazed Donut

A glazed donut is a type of yeast-raised donut, typically ring-shaped, that is coated in a sweet glaze, often made from powdered sugar and milk.

Toppings

Toppings usually consist of chocolate, cream, sprinkles, nuts, fruit, and brown sugar.
